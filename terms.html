  • No Pornography or Explicit Content: This is a public platform for all ages to enjoy. Any sexually explicit, obscene, or suggestive material will be removed immediately.
  • No Illegal Content: Content that promotes illegal activities, violence, or any other unlawful behavior is not permitted.
  • No Personal Attacks or Harassment: Respect is key. Don't post anything that harasses, bullies, or attacks other users or individuals.
  • No Commercial Spam: This platform is for art and expression, not for advertising products or services.

We reserve the right to remove any content that violates these guidelines, and repeated violations may result in your access to the platform being revoked. Let's keep it cool, respectful, and focused on art!

Gallery Moderation

When you contribute your amazing digital art to the gallery, we're thrilled to showcase it to the world. However, to ensure that the public gallery remains a safe, inspiring, and appropriate space for all viewers (especially as it will be displayed on actual screens and billboards across Sarajevo!), all submitted images will be reviewed by our team before they appear in the public gallery.

This moderation process is in place specifically to ensure compliance with our content guidelines (no hate speech, pornography, etc.). We'll do our best to get your art live as quickly as possible, but please understand that there might be a short delay between submission and publication. If an image doesn't meet our guidelines, it won't be added to the gallery.
